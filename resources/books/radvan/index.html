Further readings recommended for R users

Sunday, 11 Apr 2021 Books, Tutorials, Programming

Advanced R is a book in Chapman & Hall’s R Series. The book is designed primarily for R users who want to improve their programming skills and understanding of the language. It should also be useful for programmers coming to R from other languages, as help you to understand why R works the way it does. Links here will direct you to the 2nd edition of Advanced R. If you’re looking for the 1st edition, you can click here for more.

The website for Advanced R Solutions which provides solutions to the exercises from Hadley Wickham’s Advanced R, 2nd edition. Working through the exercises and their solutions will give you a deep understanding of a variety of programming challenges, many of which are relevant to everyday work. For the 2nd edition of the book, just click on Advanced R Solutions, while solutions to the 1st edition of Advanced R Solutions can be found at netlify.com.
